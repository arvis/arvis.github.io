Overview of game customiser.

The problem it tries to solve.

For many years, enterprises dont know how to keep users engaged. Also usrs dont like ads in general. People love to play casual games like Candy Crash and others.

Idea

The idea is customisible game templates whare customer can pick game, set his own logo/pictures somewhere and put in in interernet or App store. Users playing games can competete for prizes/ bonus points. Customer also has access to game statistics and can send various ingame messages (for example: only today all items 50% off!)

Examples

NB! All examples are on proof of conept stage and will look differently(better)

Main idea is to provide sensible variants by ourselves where customer can put logo, and use customers custom models/sounds only when he agrees to addition payment.
